No Discrimination & Privacy Policy

At S&K Medical Transportation, we are committed to providing safe, respectful, and professional non-emergency medical transportation services to every client.

Non-Discrimination Policy

We do not discriminate against any individual based on:

  • National Origin
  • Age
  • Disability
  • Color
  • Religion
  • Social Status

Every client is treated with dignity, respect, and equal access to our services.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. S&K Medical Transportation safeguards all client information, including personal and health-related data. We ensure confidentiality and secure handling of all information provided.

We provide professional door-to-door medical transportation with comfort and safety. S&K Medical Transportation offers non-emergency transportation services across Fremont, CA — available 24 hours a day, seven days a week.
