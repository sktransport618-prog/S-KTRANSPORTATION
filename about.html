ADA-Compliant Vehicles

At S&K Medical Transportation, we eliminate the struggle of patients trying to secure transportation for their medical appointments. Our accessible fleet ensures safe, smooth, and stress-free transportation.

Our specially designed vans and shuttles guarantee comfort and accessibility for wheelchair users, walker users, and able-bodied passengers.

  • Non-skid ramps for safer access
  • Power wheelchair lifts for handicapped entrance
  • Side-entry accessibility for easier sidewalk approach
  • Easy pedestrian entrance for passengers & walker users
