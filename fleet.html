OUR FLEET

Safe, Comfortable & Fully Equipped Medical Transportation Vehicles

ADA-Compliant Vehicles

At S&K Medical Transportation, we take care of the difficult task of arranging transportation for individuals with mobility challenges. We work to relieve stress by removing confusion and accommodating any difficulties that may arise during their trips to and from medical care.

Our ADA-compliant, fully customized vans and shuttles provide:

  • ✔

    Ramps with non-skid surfaces for safer access

  • ✔

    Power wheelchair lifts available for handicapped entrance

  • ✔

    Side-entry access for easier sidewalk approach

  • ✔

    Simple pedestrian entrance for able-bodied passengers & walker users

Our Comfort, Safe,
Reliable Vehicles

S&K Medical Transportation’s passion is our business, and “Service” is our motto.

We strongly believe that the safety of our healthcare partners, patients, and medical teams is critical. That is why we operate highly maintained, wheelchair-accessible vehicles designed for safe, smooth, and reliable medical transportation.

Our fleet includes wheelchair-accessible Dodge Grand Caravans (also known as wheelchair minivans) and Transit Commercial Wheelchair Transport vans — all equipped with the latest Safety Standards.

Every vehicle is GPS tracked 24/7. Speed and driver behavior are continuously monitored to ensure the highest level of safety on every trip.

We provide professional door-to-door medical transportation with comfort and safety. S&K Medical Transportation offers non-emergency transportation services across Fremont, CA — available 24 hours a day, seven days a week.
